# Product Overview: Voltwise v2.6.4-LTS
**Classification**: Institutional Decision Support Instrument  
**Governance Status**: Frozen for Review  

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Voltwise is an engineering-grade visibility instrument designed to quantify and clarify electricity cost risks for high-capacity industrial and enterprise consumers. In an increasingly volatile energy market, Voltwise serves as a **deterministic lens** through which non-deterministic cost structures become legible.

## 2. The Problem: The Fallacy of Averages
Most industrial fiscal planning relies on "Average Cost of Power." This metric is structurally flawed in liberalized markets. 
- **The Signal vs. Noise**: Volatility in the Indian Real-Time Market (RTM) is not "noise"; it is a persistent signal of grid stress.
- **The Black Swan**: A 4-hour price spike to the regulatory ceiling (₹10/kWh+) during peak production can eliminate a month’s worth of operational margin. 
Voltwise exposes these tails, allowing organizations to move from "Average-based hope" to "Tail-based literacy."

## 3. Product Philosophy: Instrument vs. Advisor
Voltwise operates on a strict **Non-Advisory Thesis**. 
- **An Instrument**: Like a cockpit altimeter, Voltwise tells the operator their current "fiscal altitude" and "risk weather." It provides high-fidelity data.
- **Not an Advisor**: Voltwise does not prescribe load shifts, suggest trading strategies, or automate dispatch. 
The system is built on the belief that **Visibility Precedes Intelligence**. By giving the operator perfect visibility into the cost-distribution floor and ceiling, the operator can make sovereign decisions.

## 4. Operational Boundaries (Visibility-Before-Intelligence)
Voltwise prioritizes transparency over "Smart" automation.
1. **Scenario Mapping**: We propagate 500 parallel futures because local certainty is impossible.
2. **Auditability**: Every pixel in the UI is traceable back to a versioned assumption in the Registry.
3. **Sovereignty**: All computation is performed locally. We do not export sensitive load profiles to external cloud providers.

## 5. Intended Audience
- **Industrial CFOs/CEOs**: For tail-risk exposure and fiscal provisioning.
- **Plant Engineers**: For counterfactual analysis of asset grounding (BESS/Renewables).
- **Technical Auditors**: For objective, reproducible cost-risk verification.
